How It Works

Participating in online auctions at The Bidder is easy! Follow these simple steps:

1. Register

Create an account to start bidding or selling your products.

2. Browse Auctions

Explore various auctions for unique and high-quality items.

3. Place a Bid

Choose an item you want and place a bid higher than the current bid.

4. Win the Auction

If you have the highest bid when the auction ends, you win!

5. Make Payment

Complete the payment securely through our platform.

6. Receive Your Item

The seller ships the product to your address after payment confirmation.
